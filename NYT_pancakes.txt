# Ingredients
- Flour, 1 cup
- Sugar, 2 spoons
- Salt, 1 teaspoon
- Baking powder, 1.5 packages
- Milk or milk alternative, 1 cup,
- Eggs, 2 (omit for vegan version, you might have to add more milk instead)
- Butter or margarine, a bit

# Preparation

1. Mix the ingredients in the presented order until you get a uniform dough.
Turn on the heater
2. Put a bit of butter in a pan and a bit of the dough.
3. Cook for around 3 minutes in each side.
